Pacman
The main protagonist. Trapped within the Eternal Maze, he seeks a way back home. 
Unfortunately, every exit appears to lead to even more maze. Gone are the familiar blue walls and the four classic ghosts he once knew (well technically, they're here with him but seem to made some new friends too). 
In the maze are laser ghosts, explosive ghosts, fire ghosts, and whatever else this nightmare can invent. At this point, escaping may be easier than understanding what's going on... if there're any exist at all.

---

Inky
Moves randomly. Whenever he reaches an intersection, he flips a coin to decide whether to continue straight or turn.

Many have tried to predict his movements. Statisticians call him chaotic. Mathematicians call him fascinating. The coin insists everything is proceeding according to plan.

---

Clyde
Always turns right.

After enough right turns, however, even Clyde gets bored and may occasionally choose left instead. He claims this is part of a sophisticated navigation strategy. Nobody believes him.

For reasons unknown, he absolutely despises Pinky.

---

Pinky
Always turns left.

Pinky considers herself a ghost of principle and consistency. Unfortunately, those principles mostly involve turning left and arguing with Clyde. Their feud has lasted so long that neither can remember what started it.

---

Blinky
He actively chases Pacman around the maze.

According to Blinky, he can smell Pacman from fifteen blocks away. Nobody knows what a ghost's nose looks like, and nobody is brave enough to ask.

---

Clony
This guy is blind... somehow

Why? Well, when a ghost loves another ghost very much...

Just kidding. Ghosts clone themselves using strange magic left behind in the maze.

Unfortunately, the cloning process appears to copy everything except eyesight and personality, despite every Clony insisting they're a completely unique individual.

Despite being totally blind, Clony charges through the maze with remarkable confidence and absolutely no sense of self-preservation.

He is also strangely unaffected by Power Pellets. As Clony himself puts it:

"You can't be scared of what you can't see."

This explanation raises several questions.

---

Lazory
Possesses a single eye and can fire deadly laser beams in a straight line.

Rumor has it he once applied to a superhero academy. Unfortunately, being deceased complicated the admissions process.

Most people, however, suspect the real reason for his rejection was that his lasers have a tendency to hit absolutely everyone, including Pacman, other ghosts, and occasionally Lazory himself and not because he's a ghost.

Icy
Slows anyone he touches and turns the ground into ice... mostly so he can slide around on it himself.

He has always dreamed of opening a ski resort somewhere inside the Eternal Maze. Winter is his favorite season, entirely unironically.

According to Icy, Pacman's inability to turn corners while sliding is simply a "skill issue."

According to Pacman, ice physics were a mistake.

He absolutely despises Infernity, who somehow manages to melt every carefully constructed ice path within minutes of its completion.

Their rivalry has caused enough property damage to qualify as a weather phenomenon.
