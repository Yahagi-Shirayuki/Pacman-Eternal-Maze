Pacman
The main protagonist. Trapped within the Eternal Maze, he seeks a way back home. Unfortunately, every exit appears to lead to even more maze. Gone are the familiar blue walls and the four classic ghosts he once knew. In their place are laser ghosts, explosive ghosts, fire ghosts, and whatever else this nightmare can invent. At this point, escaping may be easier than understanding what's going on.

---

Inky
Moves randomly. Whenever he reaches an intersection, he flips a coin to decide whether to continue straight or turn.

Many have tried to predict his movements. Statisticians call him chaotic. Mathematicians call him fascinating. The coin insists everything is proceeding according to plan.

---

Clyde
Always turns right.

After enough right turns, however, even Clyde gets bored and may occasionally choose left instead. He claims this is part of a sophisticated navigation strategy. Nobody believes him.

For reasons unknown, he absolutely despises Pinky.

---

Pinky
Always turns left.

Pinky considers herself a ghost of principle and consistency. Unfortunately, those principles mostly involve turning left and arguing with Clyde. Their feud has lasted so long that neither can remember what started it.

---

Blinky
Actively chases Pacman.

According to Blinky, he can smell Pacman from fifteen blocks away. Nobody knows what a ghost's nose looks like, and nobody is brave enough to ask.

---

Clony
Is blind.

How exactly a ghost becomes blind remains one of the Eternal Maze's greatest mysteries. Despite being unable to see anything, Clony somehow continues moving around with surprising confidence.

His success rate is significantly lower.

---

Lazory
Possesses a single eye and can fire deadly laser beams in a straight line.

Rumor has it he once applied to a superhero academy. Unfortunately, being deceased complicated the admissions process.

Most people, however, suspect the real reason for his rejection was that his lasers have a tendency to hit absolutely everyone, including Pacman, other ghosts, and occasionally Lazory himself.

---

Biggy
The mother of all ghosts. Spawns a new ghost every 50 pellets consumed (or stolen).

She is believed to be one of the very few female ghosts in the entire maze besides Pinky. How a ghost manages to create more ghosts remains a question best left to ghost scientists.

Worth 3,200 points, largely because she insists she is "a big one."

---

Sprintee
Charges forward at maximum speed until colliding with a wall.

Winner of numerous Ghost Marathon championships. Following allegations of "excessive haunting-enhanced performance," he was permanently banned from all human athletic competitions.

---

Infernity
Leaves a trail of fire wherever he travels.

Claims the flames are merely a side effect of his haunting abilities. Local fire departments remain unconvinced. If Infernity is approaching, consider relocating to somewhere less flammable.

---

Bomby
Runs directly toward Pacman and explodes on contact.

Experts recommend maintaining a safe distance. Bomby insists he's simply expressing himself. Everyone else describes it as a severe anger-management problem.

---

Magnety
Steals all pellets within a 5×5 area.

A menace to snack enthusiasts everywhere. Magnety rarely attacks directly, preferring instead to remove your food and laugh from a safe distance.

Icy
Slows anyone he touches and turns the ground into ice.

He has always dreamed of opening his own ski resort. Winter is his favorite season, entirely unironically.

He despises Infernity, who keeps melting all the ice he painstakingly puts down.
